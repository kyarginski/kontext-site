Creative group "[KonText](https://t.me/kontext_leipzig_kanal)" invites you to a reading:

**Seryozha Is Very Stupid**

**May 17 at 18:00**

Staged reading based on a play by Dmitry Danilov  
Fantastic absurdity.

Couriers from a mysterious "Delivery Service" burst into the life of Seryozha, an ordinary programmer. His steady routine, where everything is predictable, is shattered. Can life go on as before after that?

Duration: 1 hour 20 minutes (no intermission)  
Age: 14+  
Event language: Russian

Organizer: KonText, a creative theatre group from Leipzig,  
presenting staged readings in Russian.
