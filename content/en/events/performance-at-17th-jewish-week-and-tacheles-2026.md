Staged reading "Forshmak / Vorgeschmack" in Leipzig!

Creative group "KonText" invites you to a special evening where we will "listen to stories and taste life together".

Our staged reading is based on real, living interviews with our contemporaries from former Soviet republics. These are touching and funny stories about a bygone time, family, and the bond between generations. The flavors of childhood and the secrets of old family recipes are carefully preserved in these memories.

The event is held in Russian as part of the 17th Jewish Week (June 21-28) and the Saxony theme year "Tacheles 2026".

June 21, 2026 (Sunday)  
Start at 17:00  
Salon, Ariowitsch-Haus, Hinrichsenstrasse 14, 04105 Leipzig

Free entry!

Come on your own and bring your friends. Spend this warm evening with us!

Creative group "[KonText](https://t.me/kontext_leipzig_kanal)".
