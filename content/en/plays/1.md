**Creative group “KonText” invites you to a staged reading:**

**Waiting for HIM**

based on a play by Mikhail Kheifets

**29 March 2026**\
Start: **5:00 PM**

There are moments when loneliness rises like a heavy lump in the throat. Life goes on as usual, yet there is no close person nearby — the one who could understand you, support you and share your feelings.

The staged reading tells the story of four women, each with her own life and destiny. They decide to try meeting someone on the Internet. This leads them to a date in an old park…

But something goes wrong…

**A staged reading of a lyrical fantasy**
